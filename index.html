Evolve Emergency Global Trainers

Learn ACLS, BLS, and Heartsaver skills.

1. BLS (Basic Life Support)

Target audience: Healthcare providers (nurses, doctors, EMTs, medical students, etc.).
Focus: High-quality CPR (chest compressions + rescue breaths), use of AEDs (defibrillators), and managing choking.
Scenario: For patients of all ages in a clinical or pre-hospital setting.
Goal: Give the foundation of life-saving skills for professionals who may be the first responders.

2.ACLS (Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support)

Target audience: More advanced healthcare professionals (ER doctors, ICU staff, anesthesiologists, paramedics, etc.).
Focus: Builds on BLS but goes further:
Recognizing and managing cardiac arrest, stroke, and other cardiovascular emergencies.
Using advanced airway management.
Reading and interpreting ECG rhythms.
Administering emergency drugs (like epinephrine, amiodarone, atropine).
Scenario: Critical hospital or emergency settings.
Goal: Help teams make complex, time-sensitive decisions in cardiac or respiratory arrest.

3.Heartsaver


Target audience: Non-medical people (teachers, parents, security guards, coaches, babysitters, workplace employees).
Focus: Simple, easy-to-learn CPR and AED use, plus how to help choking victims.
Variations:
Heartsaver CPR AED
Heartsaver First Aid
Heartsaver Pediatric First Aid CPR AED
Scenario: Community or workplace emergencies.
Goal: Empower everyday people to save lives before medical professionals arrive.
